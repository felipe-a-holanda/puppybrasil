Roaring Penguin PPPOE


PPPoE (Point-to-Point Protocol over Ethernet) is a protocol used by many ADSL Internet Service Providers. Roaring Penguin has a free PPPoE client for Linux and Solaris systems to connect to PPPoE service providers.

Project home page: http://www.roaringpenguin.com/penguin/open_source_rp-pppoe.php

Online documentation: http://roaringpenguin.luky.org/pppoe/

Online man pages: http://www.phpman.info/index.php/man/pppoe/5
